Property

Property is a property that becomes a property after being sold to someone who desires a property, in which case the property becomes the property.


Subsale

Sub-sale properties are those that are resold by the current owner. This implies that when you purchase a sub-sale property, you are purchasing from another homeowner rather than the developer. On the other hand, new properties in the primary market are often purchased from developers.


Under-Construction

Under-construction properties are sometimes known as "sell and build" properties since the developers begin selling the property before building begins. Because the properties are in less-developed areas of the city, there is a significant chance that prices may rise as a result of future development.
