General Insurance

General insurance contracts are those that do not fall under the umbrella of life insurance.
Fine, marine, motor, accident, and other miscellaneous non-life insurance are examples of general insurance.


Takaful Malaysia General Insurance

Members donate money to a pool system to insure each other against loss or harm in this type of Islamic insurance.
Takaful insurance is founded on sharia, or Islamic religious law, which describes how individuals are obligated to work together and protect one another.
