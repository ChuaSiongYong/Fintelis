On a daily basis, interact with clients by assisting with services such as semi-loans and mortgage loans.


Semi-Loan

You may pay off a bigger portion of your outstanding loan amount in advance with Semi-Flexi Loans. Because the principle amount of your loan has been paid off quicker, your total interest will also be lower.




Mortgage-Loan

A mortgage, often known as a mortgage loan, is a contract between you (the borrower) and a mortgage lender that allows you to purchase or refinance a property without having all of the cash up front. This arrangement provides lenders the legal authority to repossess a house if you fail to satisfy the conditions of your mortgage, which most usually means not repaying the money you borrowed plus interest.
