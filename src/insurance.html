Insurance

Insurance is a method of risk management. When you buy insurance, you're buying protection against financial disasters. If anything horrible happens to you, the insurance company compensates you or someone you pick. If you do not have insurance and an accident occurs, you may be liable for all associated costs.


Life

A contract between an insurer and a policyholder is known as life insurance. In exchange for the premiums paid by the policyholder during their lifetime, a Life Insurance policy assures that the insurer will pay an amount of money to designated beneficiaries when the insured dies.


Takaful

Unlike traditional insurance, where the risk is shifted from the insured to the insurer, Takaful Insurance members share the risk. Takaful operations are founded on the mutuality concept, in which each member makes a gift to the Takaful fund.
