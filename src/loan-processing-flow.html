Step 1

Document Submission

To begin, please provide your full English name, IC number, and phone number so that we may print your most recent CCRIS report.


Step 2

Report Diagnosis

We would assess your present "bankability" after receiving your most recent report.


Step 3

Fact Finding Process

After completing the analysis of the report, our specialists would first contact you by telephone to understand your current situation and the problems you are facing. After finishing the report's analysis, our experts will call you to learn more about your present scenario and the issues you're having.


Step 4

Financial Advisory

Following that, we would conduct a one-on-one free consultation service to help you fully comprehend your present position (from the bank's perspective) and provide you with the most feasible solutions. The most essential thing is for you to understand how you may build your own credibility.


Step 5

Agreement Confirmation

After you have agreed to our consultant's suggestion, we will draught a basic agreement between us before moving forward with the loan application procedure.


Step 6

Loan Submission Process

We would compile a list of needed papers for the loan and assist you in completing the paperwork required by the bank in the quickest period possible, before submitting them to the bank for loan application.


Step 7

Contract Signing Process

Following the bank has checked your bank's reputation and papers, if there is no more rationale, the bank will accept the loan and have the applicant sign the letter offer as soon as possible after the approval.


Step 8

Bank Fund Release

The next step is to wait for the bank to distribute the loan after signing the bank agreements. The bank would deposit the loan into your account within 3 to 6 months (depending on the sort of loan you applied for and, more critically, the type of property you own.)


Step 9

Consultant Fee Collection

Our consultation charge will be assessed only after you have received a loan, so you will not be required to pay us anything prior to securing a bank loan.
